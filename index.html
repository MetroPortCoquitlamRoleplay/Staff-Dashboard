By logging into the Staff Portal, you agree to the following terms and conditions. These apply to all staff members regardless of rank or role.

  • 2
    All Staff Portal information is strictly confidential and must remain protected at all times, including after resignation or retirement.
  • 3
    Access to the Staff Portal is granted solely for authorized staff use and must not be used for personal purposes.

    Any misuse or violation of these terms may result in disciplinary action, immediate termination, and/or a permanent blacklist.
